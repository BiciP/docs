## What is a ChatHive agent

The ChatHive agent is a an AI chatbot that is deeply fine-tuned to act as a professional customer support agent. This gives it abilities to answer customer questions, execute custom actions and detect bad actors.\
We use state-of-the-art AI models to get the best possible performance.
